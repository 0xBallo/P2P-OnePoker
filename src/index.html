Game Description

OnePoker is a turn-based game where you have four pile of cards and you have to make the greatest score possible in every piles, flipping one card a time and putting it in a pile you choose.

Game ends when you reach 502 points or you get an Instant Win.

Instant Win

If you get Four of a kind on the first turn, you Instant Win the game!
